Alamein International University (AIU)

A National university directly affiliated with the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research.
Established according to the presidential Decision No. 435 of 2020.


Located in New Alamein City, Matruh Government on an area of 150 acres, it includes a central library, an outdoor theater, a sports and recreational area, a university hospital, a dental hospital, and a university administration building. Additionally, there are 10 buildings for the colleges, 4 residential towers for students and faculty members, and staff, in addition to 9 additional residential buildings outside the university campus in the housing district. Construction has been completed at a rate of 50%.
