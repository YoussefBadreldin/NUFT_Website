Calculate Your Score for Egyptian Universities

Important Notes

For Public universities:

  • - If your SAT 1 score is above 1090, the calculation is: (your SAT 1 score/1600) *69 + (your SAT 2 score/1600) *15 + GPA.
  • - You must get at least 1050 score in SAT 1.
  • - To add the SAT 2 score, you must get at least 1100 score.
  • - The bonus for getting a score above 1090 is 9%.


For private & national universities:

  • - If your SAT 1 score is above 1090, the calculation is: (your SAT 1 score/1600) *75 + (your SAT 2 score/1600) *15 + GPA.
  • - To add the SAT 2 score, you must get at least 900 score.
  • - The bonus for getting above 1090 is 15%.


For higher institutes:

  • - You must get at least 890 score in SAT 1.
  • - To add the SAT 2 score, you must get at least 900 score.


General notes:

  • - If your SAT 1 score is below 1090, the calculation is: (your SAT 1 score/1600) *60 + (your SAT 2 score/1600) *15 + GPA.
  • - SAT 2 score will be calculated as if it's equal to zero if it's less than 900 for private universities and higher institutes or 1100 for Public universities.
  • - The bonus is added automatically to your total score if your SAT 1 score is above 1090.
  • - To get accurate results, you must follow the scoring rules carefully.
